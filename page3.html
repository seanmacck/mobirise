Generative Audio Visual Display
By Seán Mckinley

  • Proccessing
  • C++
  • Java
  • Interactive

The Audio-visual aesthetic I aimed to render was a sort of place or area in outer space. My aim was to create a black space background full of moving stars, and a planet of some sort located in the centre of it all. I had a look at some generative artworks created and developed by various artists that portrayed some interesting ideas that aided me in developing my idea.

My concept for the algorithm is to create a particle system thats noise driven, to portray particles or stars moving and/or interacting in outer space. The minim library in processing has useful examples for mapping sound and visuals together, which suited the way I aimed to render my art piece.
